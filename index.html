Jan's Fantastic Paradise

Lusciousness all around

Jan Sengers (25) would like to call themself an artistic designer – something in between arts and design. During their studies in interior, furniture, and product design they noticed that coming up with a conceptual design, or concepts for that matter, is what interests them the most. This can either be in the form of an object, a space, or both. They are interested in exhibition design that showcases projects in an exciting way while marrying it with the given space.
